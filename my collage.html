Pimpri Chinchwad University: A Hub of Innovation

Pimpri Chinchwad University (PCU) is a leading institution in the Pune region, known for its commitment to academic excellence and innovation. With a diverse range of programs spanning engineering, management, and the sciences, PCU provides students with a comprehensive educational experience. The university's state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty create an environment conducive to learning and growth. PCU also emphasizes research and development, fostering a culture of innovation among its students and faculty. Whether you're a prospective student or a member of the PCU community, you'll find a dynamic and enriching environment that prepares you for success in the 21st century.

Pimpri Chinchwad Education Trust (PCET) has a legacy of nurturing excellence since its establishment in 1990. At PCET, we aim to ignite the spark of knowledge in every student. Our 11 institutes across our 3 campuses in Nigdi, Ravet, and Sate have carved out a name for themselves, having transformed the lives of over 46,000 students so far. Our institutes, including Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering (PCCOE), one of the top Engineering Colleges in Pune, Pune Business School, and S.B. Patil College of Architecture and Design, are some of the most sought-after colleges in India. Pimpri Chinchwad University (PCU), under the aegis of PCET, has set its aim high to transform students into trailblazers of tomorrow. Set on a sprawling 10-acre campus in the bustling Pune Metropolitan Area, our university boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure to inspire and empower students and teachers alike.
